DIY Ayurvedic Hair Oil

Most Economical
Cost: Half the price of commercial oils

Harmful Ingredients to Avoid

5 Ingredients
☣️

Mineral Oil / Light Liquid Paraffin (LLP)

Carcinogenic Risk
Petroleum industry byproduct. May contain carcinogenic polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons unless labeled "white mineral oil" or "white petroleum" (which no Indian brand mentions). Found in 70-80% of commercial hair oils.
☣️

BHT (Butylated Hydroxytoluene)

Harmful
Harmful preservative found in Wow anti-hairfall oil. Can damage hair over time.
☣️

Artificial Fragrance / Sugandh Dravya

Irritant
Unnecessary chemicals that can irritate scalp. Found in Patanjali oils, old Mamaearth formulas, and Indulekha Sua Kutaja.
☣️

Cosmetic Colors

No Benefit
No benefit, potential harm to hair and scalp. Found in many Patanjali variants.
☣️

Harsh Preservatives & Chemical Names

Harmful
Can damage hair over time. Check labels for unpronounceable chemical names and reject products containing them.
